Help & Features Guide

Events

Use the "Current Event" dropdown to switch between event presets (e.g., "Castle Battle"). All your teams and player roles are saved separately for each event.

Importing

Use the "Quick Start" steps to import your roster from a CSV. This will add all players to the "My Roster" list for all events, ready to be assigned.

Managing Teams

  • Add Team: Click the + button to create a new, empty team for the current event.
  • Edit Title: Click any team's title ("New Team") to rename it. Click away to save.
  • Edit Description: Click the large text area to add notes. It saves when you click away.
  • Delete Team: Click the red X on a team to delete it for this event. All players will be safely moved back to "My Roster".

Managing Players

  • Move Player: Click and drag any player from the roster to a team, between teams, or back to the roster.
  • Set Role: Right-click a player's name to open the role menu. You can set them as a "Group Leader", "Rally Leader", or "Member".
  • Pinning: Leaders and Rally Leaders are automatically "pinned" to the top of their team list.

Utilities

  • Clear All Teams: This red button will move *all* players for the *current event* back to "My Roster" and reset their roles to "Member".
  • Export Roster: This downloads a CSV file of all players, showing their assigned team and role *for the current event only*.
